When a field with the same name as the @Id field is added to the governor, the @Id field in the entity ITD has its name changed by prefixing it with an underscore ("_") but the the field name referred to in the find call in the remove() method still has the un-prefixed field name. This can be fixed by simply querying the name of the current identifier field with getIdentifierField() rather than "identifierField" which is used presently
