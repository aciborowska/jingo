Due to reported problems with perm gen space, I suggest to switch the JVM flags from:


	`-XX:+UseParNewGC`
to
	`-XX:+UseConcMarkSweepGC -XX:+CMSClassUnloadingEnabled -XX:MaxPermSize=256m`
