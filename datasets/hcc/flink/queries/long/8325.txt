COUNT(1) with Group Window, always output 0. 
e.g.
DATA:


val data = List(
    (1L, 1, "Hi"),
    (2L, 2, "Hello"),
    (4L, 2, "Hello"),
    (8L, 3, "Hello world"),
    (16L, 3, "Hello world"))



SQL:


SELECT b, COUNT(1) FROM MyTable GROUP BY Hop(proctime, interval '0.001' SECOND, interval '0.002' SECOND),b



OUTPUT:


1,0,1, 
1,0,1, 
2,0,1,
2,0,1, 
2,0,2, 
3,0,1,
3,0,1
