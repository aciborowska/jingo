When the execution graph creation has long calls (or blocking calls), it brings down the cluster. It seems to block the main actor thread of the dispatcher, making the entire cluster unresponsive (web UI, etc.)

Reproducing

One way to reproduce this is to use a FileSystem that blocks on initialization or on opening a stream.
