The snippet below will cause a parser exception.


 testAllApis(
      concat_ws("~", "AA", "\\"),
      "concat_ws('~','AA','\\')",
      "concat_ws('~','AA','\\\\')",
      "AA~\\")




org.apache.flink.table.api.ExpressionParserException: Could not parse expression at column 20: Invalid expression.
concat_ws('~','AA','\')
                   ^

However, with double (or more) backslashes, it will be successfully parsed.


testAllApis(
      concat_ws("~", "AA", "\\\\"),
      "concat_ws('~','AA','\\\\')",
      "concat_ws('~','AA','\\\\\\\\')",
      "AA~\\\\")
