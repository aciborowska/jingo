Following the quickstart guide for snapshot versions, the dependencies cannot be resolved, because the snapshot repositories are missing:


[ERROR] Failed to execute goal
org.apache.maven.plugins:maven-archetype-plugin:2.3:generate (default-cli)
on project standalone-pom: The desired archetype does not exist
(org.apache.flink:flink-quickstart-scala:1.1-SNAPSHOT) -> [Help 1]



We have to add the following to the mvn command here https://ci.apache.org/projects/flink/flink-docs-master/quickstart/scala_api_quickstart.html#maven (and for Java):


 -DarchetypeVersion=1.1-SNAPSHOT \
   -DarchetypeCatalog=https://repository.apache.org/content/repositories/snapshots/
