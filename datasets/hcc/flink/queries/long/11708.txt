Currently TaskExecutor.start() method starts by connecting to the ResourceManager and ends with a startRegistrationTimeout() method. However, the startRegistrationTimeout() is probably called after the connection to ResourceManager is established. Then in time of "taskmanager.registration.timeout", registrationTimeout() is called and throw a RegistrationTimeoutException bellow, which causes the TaskExecutor shutdown:
