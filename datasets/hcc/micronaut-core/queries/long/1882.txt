### Steps to Reproduce

1. Create a project with `max-request-size: 50mb`
2. Create `@Post/@Put` method with `@Body` parameter
3. Invoke with 50mb content

### Expected Behaviour

Call is successful

### Actual Behaviour

`io.micronaut.http.client.exceptions.HttpClientResponseException: The content length [1055904] exceeds the maximum allowed content length [1048576]`

With `multipart.max-file-size: 50mb` works ok. 

### Environment Information

- **Micronaut Version:** : 1.2.0.RC2
