### Steps to Reproduce

1. create app for kotlin with swagger
2. set `kotlinVersion` to `1.3.21` in `gradle.properties`
2. try to compile

Solution for me is to change `@Info` to `Info` in `Application.kt`.  I see the correctly generated swagger YAML after that.
