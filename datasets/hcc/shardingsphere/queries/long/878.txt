## 版本：2.0.3
```sql
SELECT d.*
FROM event e
    , detector_device d
where e.status = #{handleStatus}
      and d.is_online not in (2, 3)
      and e.device_code = d.code
group by e.device_code
order by e.date desc
```
正确执行
