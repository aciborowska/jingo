### Environment
MM 46.1

### Description
When BA Tube Artillery hits it is only doing 3 damage total.  Total damage from a squad attack is supposed to be multiplied by the total number of men in the squad.  (Thus a full squad of 4 would do 12 damage per attack.)

In addition Its an AOE attack, that does 3xMen damage in the impact hex, and 1xMen damage in the surrounding 6 hexes.

TO, pg 284, or the TO errata document pg 49.
