Under TacOps rules, battlemechs follow the same rules for heat generation for industrialmechs when using I.C.E. or Fuel Cells. However, when using Fuel Cells in battlemechs, heat seem to be generated by movement in game. Rule clarification regarding I.C.E./Fuel Cell mechs was addressed here: http://bg.battletech.com/forums/index.php?topic=36206.0
