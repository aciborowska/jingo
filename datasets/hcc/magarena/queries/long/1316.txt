Hour of Promise doesn't create tokens if player didn't have 3 Deserts before casting.

- In play: 3x Forest, Hashep Oasis, Sunscorched Desert
- Cast Hour of Promise, choose Hostile Desert and Grasping Dunes from library
- No tokens are created, even through player controls 4 Deserts
- Now go back to first step. Play Dunes of the Dead from hand, then cast Hour of Promise and make the same selections. Tokens are created.
