**Describe the bug**
When a customer provides a note when placing an order, that note shows up in two different sections on the fulfillment screen.

**To Reproduce**
Steps to reproduce the behavior:
1. Place an order with a note.
2. View the order in the app.
3. Tap Begin Fulfillment.
4. See the duplicated note.

**Screenshots**
![image000000-1](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/373903/73784530-4301aa00-475b-11ea-93fc-491394127e05.jpeg)

**Expected behavior**
Note should only show up in one place.

**Mobile Environment**
Please include:
- Device: Pixel 2 XL
- Android version: 10
- WooCommerce Android version: 3.5-rc-2
