#2814150-zen

**Describe the bug**
When editing a product that does not have weights assigned, the app places 0.00 in for weights and dimensions and then shows that information on the front end. This does not happen in the iOS app. User reported and I was able to replicate exactly the same way.

**To Reproduce**
Steps to reproduce the behavior:
1. Create a simple product - do not assign weights and dimensions
2. Confirm on front end of site that none appear
3. Open app, go to product, make change (I toggled on the sale option)
4. Save/confirm/update the product
5. Look at the front end and see that the details have been added.
6. Note that the weights/dimensions do not appear in the app, but are showing on the front end

**Screenshots**
![](https://d.pr/i/DKw7te+)Image Link: https://d.pr/i/DKw7te

**Expected behavior**
Not specifically adding a weight/dimension means they should not appear

- [x ] I have deactivated other plugins and confirmed this bug occurs when only WooCommerce plugin is active. (along with Jetpack per use of the app)
- [ x] This bug happens with a default WordPress theme active, or [Storefront](https://woocommerce.com/storefront/).
- [x ] I can reproduce this bug consistently using the steps above.
