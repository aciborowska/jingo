**Describe the bug**
If the site has an extension installed to allow order quantity of a product in decimal, the product will not show up at the order details screen if the quantity is less than 1 (e.g. 0.3).

If the quantity is more than 1, the product shows up without the decimal quantity.

Reported in 3302211-zen.

**To Reproduce**

Pre-requisite: An extension that enabled decimal quantities should be installed and activated. 

Steps to reproduce the behavior:
1. Create an order with a product that has the quantity of less than 1 (e.g. 0.3)
2. Create another order with a product that has a quantity of 1.1
3. On the app, go to 'Orders'
4. Tap on the order created in (1)
5. See that the Product section is missing
6. Go back to the Orders screen and tap on the order created in (2)
7. See that the product quantity showing at the Product section is in integer (i.e. 1)

The product should be displayed on the order details screen with the correct product quantity in decimal.
