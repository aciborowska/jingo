**Describe the bug**

On a small device screen, review moderation buttons (trash, spam, approve) don't fit on screen if you're using a larger font size or a language with longer strings. The buttons don't scroll or otherwise respond to the available space.

**To Reproduce**

1. On a small device, set the font size to the largest available size or switch to a language with longer strings (e.g. Spanish or German).
2. In the app, open a review.
3. Scroll down to the moderation buttons and notice that they are pushed off the left side of the screen.
