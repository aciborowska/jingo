**Describe the bug**

When you try to upload an image to a product, the 'done' button does not show up on the Photos page until you first select an image to view it full screen and return. 

**To Reproduce**
Steps to reproduce the behavior:
1. Go to 'Products', Select a product and click on the icon to upload a new image.
2. Click on Add Photos and select any of the options to add a photo. I select 'choose from device'.
3. Select a picture and upload it to the product.
4. You will be returned to the Photos page, but the thumbnail for the image doesn't always display(this is inconsistent but happens only when the choose from device options is used). The done button never displays.
5. Click on the existing image thumbnail to open it in the lightbox.
6. Return to the Photos page, the 'Done' button displays and you can now update the product.
