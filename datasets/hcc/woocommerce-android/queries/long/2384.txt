**Describe the bug**

When the Help & Support > My Tickets screen is empty (no existing tickets), the following text is unreadable in dark theme:

> Conversations started from this app will be visible here.

**To Reproduce**

1. Go to Settings > Appearance (in the app).
2. Enable Dark theme.
3. Go to Help & Support > My tickets.
4. If you have any open support conversations on this screen, you can log out of the app to clear them. After you log out, go back to Help > My tickets.
5. Notice the explanatory text is white on a grey background.
