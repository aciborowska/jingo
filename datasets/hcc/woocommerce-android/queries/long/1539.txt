**Describe the bug**

When I enter an amount in the manual refund amount field, and then backspace to remove a character, it deletes 2 characters at a time instead of 1.

**To Reproduce**
Steps to reproduce the behavior:
1. Go to the Orders tab.
2. Select a completed order.
3. Scroll down to the Payment section and tap Issue Refund.
4. Enter an amount for the refund.
5. Tap the backspace button once.
6. Notice that the last 2 numbers were deleted.
