**Describe the bug**

After issuing a refund, selecting that refund to view the refund detail displays the placeholder `%s` instead of the store admin link, and the shipping amount is not displayed.

**To Reproduce**
Steps to reproduce the behavior:
1. Go to the Orders tab.
2. Open a refunded order (or issue a refund on an existing order).
3. On the order detail, tap on the refund line item to view the refund detail.
