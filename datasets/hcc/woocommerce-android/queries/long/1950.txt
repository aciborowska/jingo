**Describe the bug**

The overflow menu (Help/Support) on the Reviews tab can disappear if you rotate the device while viewing review details or after opening review details from a push notification.

**To Reproduce**
Steps to reproduce the behavior:
1. Open the Reviews tab and notice the overflow menu appears in the top right.
2. Tap on a review to open the review details.
3. Rotate your device between portrait and landscape.
4. Go back to the reviews list and notice the overflow menu is gone.
5. Tap to another tab in the bottom nav and tap back to the Reviews tab, and notice the overflow menu is back.

**Expected behavior**

I expect the overflow menu to always be visible on the Reviews tab.

**Isolating the problem (mark completed items with an [x]):**

- [x] This bug happens with a default WordPress theme active, or [Storefront](https://woocommerce.com/storefront/).
- [x] I can reproduce this bug consistently using the steps above.
