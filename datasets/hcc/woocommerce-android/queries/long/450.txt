### Expected
The ***Filter*** action is not shown on the ***Order Details*** screen.

### Observed
The ***Filter*** action is shown on the ***Order Details*** screen when tapping the overflow menu action.

### Reproduced
1. Go to ***Orders*** tab.
2. Select order from list.
3. Notice ***Filter*** action is hidden.
4. Tap menu overflow action.
5. Tap outside popup menu.
6. Notice ***Filter*** action is shown.
7. Tap ***Filter*** action.
8. Select filter in dialog.
9. Tap ***Apply Filter*** button.
10. Notice screen doesn't change.

##### Tested
Google Pixel 3 on Android 9.0 with WooCommerce 0.18
