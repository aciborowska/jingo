This simple XQuery will lead to a StackOverflowError:

```xquery
3 treat as item()+ + +1
```

The result should instead be `4`.

This is taken from the XQTS 3.1.0:

```xml
  <test-case name="K2-SeqExprTreat-2">
      <description> Use three pluses on a row, combined with 'treat as'. </description>
      <created by="Frans Englich" on="2007-11-26"/>
      <test>3 treat as item()+ + +1</test>
      <result>
         <assert-eq>4</assert-eq>
      </result>
   </test-case>
```
