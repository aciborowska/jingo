spec-> http://www.w3.org/TR/xpath-functions-30/#func-filter

```
fn:filter($seq as item()*, $f as function(item()) as xs:boolean) as item()*
```

exist --> http://exist-db.org/exist/apps/fundocs/view.html?uri=http://www.w3.org/2005/xpath-functions&location=java:org.exist.xquery.functions.fn.FnModule 

```
filter($function as function, $sequence as item()*) as item()*
```
