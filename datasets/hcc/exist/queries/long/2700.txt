### What is the problem

When using the facets feature in 5.0.0 (develop) with stock TEI Publisher (master), I am hitting a “this IndexReader is closed” error when loading a page that queries facets during a reindex. For example, running `xmldb:reindex("/db/apps/tei-publisher")` and then requesting http://localhost:8080/exist/apps/tei-publisher/modules/facets.xql?sort=title before the reindex is complete will yield a 500 error, culminating in:

```
org.apache.lucene.store.AlreadyClosedException: this IndexReader is closed
    at org.apache.lucene.index.IndexReader.ensureOpen(IndexReader.java:276)
    at org.apache.lucene.index.SegmentReader.getBinaryDocValues(SegmentReader.java:501)
    at org.apache.lucene.facet.taxonomy.FastTaxonomyFacetCounts.count(FastTaxonomyFacetCounts.java:54)
    at org.apache.lucene.facet.taxonomy.FastTaxonomyFacetCounts.<init>(FastTaxonomyFacetCounts.java:49)
    at org.apache.lucene.facet.taxonomy.FastTaxonomyFacetCounts.<init>(FastTaxonomyFacetCounts.java:39)
    at org.exist.indexing.lucene.LuceneIndexWorker.lambda$getFacets$3(LuceneIndexWorker.java:554)
    at org.exist.indexing.lucene.LuceneIndex.withSearcher(LuceneIndex.java:259)
    at org.exist.indexing.lucene.LuceneIndexWorker.getFacets(LuceneIndexWorker.java:554)
    at org.exist.xquery.modules.lucene.Facets.addFacetsToMap(Facets.java:151)
    at org.exist.xquery.modules.lucene.Facets.eval(Facets.java:141)
```

This seems to describe the problem and a possible solution too:

https://stackoverflow.com/questions/41437582/lucene-alreadyclosedexception-this-indexreader-is-closed

### What did you expect

I expected to be able to make requests to the application during reindexing without encountering a 500 error. I hope this is surmountable. Otherwise, production instances cannot perform reindexing or even perhaps loading large amounts of data.
