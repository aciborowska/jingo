Concurrently creating and destroying `Cache` proxies of the same cache is racy and may result in failures. There is already a [test](https://github.com/vbekiaris/hazelcast/blob/65561917a88041145062ae0edc206a17727dac6d/hazelcast/src/test/java/com/hazelcast/cache/CacheDestroyTest.java#L197) in place which demonstrates the issue.
