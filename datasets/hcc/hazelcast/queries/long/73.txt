That sample code below gives you a result of: 1,2,3,0 
in place of the right one: 0,1,2,3.

Build the attache project and drop the war into 
your local Tomcat, now go to --&gt; http://localhost:8080/hazelcast-sample/

```
   IList list = hazelcast.getList(&quot;default&quot;);
   list.clear();

   Transaction txn1 = hazelcast.getTransaction();
   txn1.begin();
   list.add(&quot;1&quot;);
   list.add(&quot;2&quot;);
   list.add(&quot;3&quot;);
   txn1.commit();

   Transaction txn2 = hazelcast.getTransaction();
   txn2.begin();
   list.add(0, &quot;0&quot;);
   txn2.commit();
```
