To demonstrate this, I've launched just a single hazelcast **3.12.1** member.
When connecting it to a **3.12.2** hazelcast management center, the map statistics data are different than when connecting it to a 3.12.1 hazelcast management center.
The columns displayed in the map statistics differ between both versions, resulting in data displayed in the wrong column when using hazelcast: events are displayed in the locks column.
