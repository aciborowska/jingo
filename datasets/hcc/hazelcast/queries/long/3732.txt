After upgrading from hz 3.1.6 to 3.3, our hazelcast environments can no longer start up successfully. They get stuck in a loop throwing HazelcastInstanceNotActiveException. The environments share a multicast address and multicast port but have a different group-name. As of 3.3 it seems the discovery process gets stuck when it sees multicast traffic from a different group name.

We worked around the problem by giving each environment a unique multicast address, and then everything works correctly again. Needless to say, this is an unpleasant regression and would be good if it can be fixed.
