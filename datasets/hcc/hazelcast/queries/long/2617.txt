`CacheEntry` was a class through 4.1, but from 4.2 on it is now an interface. As a result the reflection being done in [Hibernate4CacheEntrySerializer](https://github.com/hazelcast/hazelcast/blob/master/hazelcast-hibernate/hazelcast-hibernate4/src/main/java/com/hazelcast/hibernate/serialization/Hibernate4CacheEntrySerializer.java#L50)'s static initializer fails on newer versions of Hibernate.
