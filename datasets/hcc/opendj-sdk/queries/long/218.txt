Matt is correct.
The (all) permission grants right to ALL basic operations (Add, Delete, Modify, Read, Search) but not the Proxy, Import, Export : this behavior is inherited from Sun DS ACI syntax and behavior. 

You need to explicitely grant the Proxy permission :
... allow (all, proxy) userdn="ldap:///cn=appuser,ou=Administrators,dc=mycompany,dc=com"
