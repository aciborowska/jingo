Under load the changelog indexer can lag behind, making the number of changelog files quite large. In an overnight test we observed a bit more than 6900 files (as mentioned in OPENDJ-2182) and in another instance more than 10K.
Looking at stack traces from OPENDJ-2182, we noticed cursors for some domains (cn=schema, cn=admin data) are systematically kept in the list of exhausted cursors, while the cursor for user data seems to scan all files from the beginning. The double "recursive" call to addCursor() seems strange...
