Some objectclasses (at least slpService, slpServicePrinter, printerAbstract, printerServiceAuxClass, printerIPP, printerLPR) are considered STRUCTURAL although defined as AUXILIARY or ABSTRACT. The reason is that this type is set before SUP ... in those ldif entries rather than after SUP ..., as should be (per RFC 4512, section 4.1.1).
