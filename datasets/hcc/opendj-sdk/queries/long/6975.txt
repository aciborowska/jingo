It appears that the Proxy is forwarding the CLIENT_SIDE_* ResultCode resulting from its communication with the underlying DS up to the end client.

There ResultCode are not intended to be transmitted on the wire and must be substituted by the Proxy with standard LDAP ResultCode
 Example: CLIENT_SIDE_CONNECT_ERROR and CLIENT_SIDE_SERVER_DOWN should be translated into UNAVAILABLE

Acceptance criteria:

	CLIENT_SIDE_* result codes are never sent to the end client
