With following option set...
ds-cfg-invalid-attribute-syntax-behavior: accept

The OpenDJ 3.0 excludes from searches uniquemember values that don't conform to the correct syntax.
For example...

dn: cn=xyz
objectClass: groupofuniquenames
...
uniquemember: somejunk
uniquemember: somejunk2
uniquemember: somejunk3
...



a filter of (uniquemember=somejunk) returns nothing.
