This is the parent task for OpenJPA iteration 1.5 (aka. holiday iteration). The iteration 1.5 development period is from Dec. 22nd - Jan 2nd. Features to be completed during this iteration must be assigned as a subtask to this task. The JPA 2.0 Roadmap [1] contains iteration 1 features and tracking information.
