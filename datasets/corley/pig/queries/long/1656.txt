TOBAG udf ignores columns with null value

R4= foreach B generate $0,  TOBAG( id, null, id,null );
grunt> dump R4;
1000    {(1),(1)}
1000    {(2),(2)}
1000    {(3),(3)}
1000    {(4),(4)}
