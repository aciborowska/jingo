Given the Avro schema:


{"type":"record",
 "name":"toplevel",
 "fields":[{"name":"a","
            "type":{"type":"record",
                    "name":"x",
                    "fields":[{"name":"key","type":"int"},
                              {"name":"value","type":"string"}]}},
           {"name":"b","type":"x"}]}



we should get back the Pig schema


 {a: (key: int,value: string),b:(key: int,value: string)} 

but instead it is


 {a: (key: int,value: string),b: bytearray}
